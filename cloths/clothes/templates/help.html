FAQs

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Go to “My Orders” in your account dashboard and click “Track” next to your order.

Q: Can I return or exchange an item?
A: Yes! We offer hassle-free returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn and with tags.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3–5 working days across India. Express options are also available at checkout.

Returns & Exchanges

To initiate a return, visit your order history, click on the item, and select “Return or Exchange.” You’ll receive pickup and refund instructions within 24 hours.

Shipping Details

We ship to all major cities and towns across India. Orders placed before 3 PM are usually processed the same day.

Customer Care Hours

We’re available Monday to Saturday from 9 AM to 7 PM IST. For urgent queries, please email or call directly.
